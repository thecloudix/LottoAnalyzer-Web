Lotto Analyzer

Version 1.0.9

A tool for exploring historical Powerball drawing data — frequency, due numbers, scoring, and tournament-style suggestions.

This is an educational analyzer, not a gambling app. It does not predict winning numbers and does not improve your odds. Powerball draws are independent random events.
